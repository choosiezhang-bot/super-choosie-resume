# OpenAI — AI Deployment Engineer (Sydney) — Work Requirements (Clean Summary)

Source JD: https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/openai/83730f3e-3a96-476e-afb0-15f1f045ab03

## Mission (what success looks like)

Help strategic enterprise customers **safely deploy Generative AI** and achieve **tangible business value** by turning high-ROI use cases into production systems on **ChatGPT / OpenAI API**.

## Core responsibilities (what you will do)

1) **Embed with strategic customers** as the technical lead / thought partner
- Act as the “go-to” technical owner for a portfolio of key customers.
- Co-design novel GenAI applications on OpenAI’s API.

2) **Shape GenAI strategy + qualify a backlog** with senior stakeholders
- Run discovery with senior business + technical leaders.
- Identify and prioritize the **highest value** GenAI applications for the customer’s industry.
- Turn ideas into an actionable roadmap/backlog with clear success metrics.

3) **Accelerate time-to-value** via hands-on prototypes and/or high-leverage guidance
- Build prototypes when needed.
- Provide architecture decisions, best practices, and unblock technical teams.

4) **Drive prototype → production → scale**
- Design end-to-end enterprise architecture (cloud/network/security/data constraints).
- Ensure deployments are safe, effective, and scalable.
- Manage stakeholder relationships to ensure adoption and expansion.

5) **Scale impact beyond 1:1 customer work**
- Contribute to open-source / developer resources.
- Codify best practices; publish notebooks and reusable assets.
- Synthesize high-signal customer feedback for Product + Research.

## What the role is optimized for (signals the team likely values)

- **Trusted advisor** energy: crisp communication with senior stakeholders.
- **Solutions leader** with product mindset: outcomes, adoption, ROI.
- **Hands-on builder**: ability to prototype and/or productionize.
- **Enterprise architecture** fluency: security, governance, network, cloud.
- **High-throughput operator**: run multiple workstreams; prioritize ruthlessly.

## Minimum/target profile (from JD)

- ~**6+ years** technical consulting (or equivalent), bridging business + technical stakeholders.
- Experience leading complex GenAI / ML implementations.
- Programming experience (explicitly calls out **Python or JavaScript**).
